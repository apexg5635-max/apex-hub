About Apex Hub

Chhattisgarh's Most Trusted Coaching Platform Since 2018

Our Story

Apex Hub was born in 2018 with a simple yet powerful vision: every student in Chhattisgarh deserves access to world-class coaching, regardless of their location or financial background.

Founded by a group of IITians, doctors, and civil servants from Chhattisgarh, we understood the challenges faced by students in Tier-2 and Tier-3 cities. Limited access to quality faculty, expensive coaching fees, and the need to relocate to metro cities were barriers that prevented talented students from achieving their dreams.

Starting with just 50 students and 3 faculty members in a small room in Raipur, Apex Hub has now grown into Chhattisgarh's largest online coaching platform with 5000+ active students, 50+ expert faculty, and 200+ selections in government jobs, medical, and engineering exams.

Our Mission

To democratize competitive exam preparation by providing affordable, high-quality, bilingual coaching to every student in Chhattisgarh and beyond.

Our Vision

To become India's most trusted regional coaching platform, producing 1000+ government officers, doctors, and engineers from Chhattisgarh every year by 2030.

Our Values

Student-first approach, uncompromising quality, affordability, innovation in teaching, and deep commitment to Chhattisgarh's educational development.
