Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Apex Hub courses and platform

What is Apex Hub and what courses do you offer?
Apex Hub is Chhattisgarh's premier online coaching platform. We offer comprehensive courses for CG PSC (CGPSC), CG Vyapam (all posts), NEET (Medical), and JEE (Main + Advanced). All courses include live interactive classes, recorded lectures, study material, test series, and doubt resolution.
How do live classes work? What if I miss a class?
Live classes are conducted on our secure platform (Zoom/Google Meet integrated). You'll receive class links 30 minutes before the session via email, WhatsApp, and dashboard. All live classes are recorded and uploaded within 2 hours. You can watch them anytime from your dashboard. Recordings remain accessible until your course validity expires.
What is the course fee and are there any EMI options?
Course fees vary by program. CG Vyapam courses start at ₹1,499, CG PSC at ₹3,499, NEET at ₹3,999, and JEE at ₹4,999. We offer EMI options starting at just ₹499/month through our payment partners. We also provide scholarship discounts based on merit-cum-means and early-bird offers. Contact our counsellor for personalized pricing.
Can I get a demo class before enrolling?
Absolutely! We offer 3 free demo classes for every course. You can experience our teaching quality, platform features, and interact with faculty before making any payment. Click the "Free Demo Class" button on our website or WhatsApp us to schedule your demo.
Is the course content bilingual (Hindi + English)?
Yes! All our courses are taught in a Hindi-English mix, specifically designed for Chhattisgarh students. Study material is available in both Hindi and English. You can choose your preferred language for notes and tests. This bilingual approach ensures complete conceptual clarity.
What is your refund policy?
We offer a 7-day no-questions-asked refund policy from the batch start date. If you're not satisfied with the course quality, you'll get a full refund within 7 working days. After 7 days, refunds are processed on a pro-rata basis. No refunds are available after 30% of the course is completed. Refer to our Refund Policy page for detailed terms.
How do I resolve doubts during the course?
You can ask doubts in multiple ways: (1) During live classes using the chat feature, (2) In the dedicated WhatsApp group for your batch, (3) On the Doubt Forum in your dashboard, (4) During weekly doubt-clearing sessions. Faculty typically responds within 2-4 hours, with a maximum response time of 24 hours.
Are the test series similar to actual exam patterns?
Yes, our test series is meticulously designed to match the exact pattern, difficulty level, and syllabus of the respective exams. We analyze previous year trends and update our tests accordingly. For NEET and JEE, we provide All India Rank predictions. For CG PSC and Vyapam, we provide state-level rank analysis.
What devices can I use to access the courses?
You can access Apex Hub on any device — laptop, desktop, tablet, or smartphone. Our platform is fully responsive. We recommend using a laptop/desktop for an optimal learning experience, especially for test series. Mobile users can install our website as a PWA (Progressive Web App) for easy access.
Do you provide placement assistance or interview guidance?
For CG PSC and CG Vyapam courses, we provide complete interview guidance including mock interviews, personality development sessions, and current affairs preparation. While we don't guarantee job placement (as selection depends on exam performance), we provide all necessary support until your final selection.
