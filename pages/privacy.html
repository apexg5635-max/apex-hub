1. Privacy Policy

At Apex Hub ("we," "our," or "us"), we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website apexhub.in or use our services.

By accessing or using our website and services, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with any part of this policy, please discontinue use of our services immediately.

We never sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

We may collect the following personal information when you register, enroll in a course, or contact us:

  • Identity Information: Full name, date of birth, gender
  • Contact Information: Email address, phone number, residential address, state, city
  • Academic Information: Educational qualifications, exam preferences, previous scores
  • Payment Information: Billing address, payment method details (processed securely through third-party payment gateways; we do NOT store full credit card numbers)
  • Account Credentials: Username and encrypted password

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

When you visit our website, we automatically collect:

  • IP address and device information
  • Browser type and version
  • Pages visited and time spent on pages
  • Referring website URLs
  • Course progress and test performance data
  • Live class attendance records

2.3 Information from Third Parties

We may receive information about you from:

  • Payment processors (Razorpay, Instamojo) for transaction verification
  • Social media platforms if you interact with our pages
  • Referral programs when referred by existing students

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  1. Service Delivery: To provide access to courses, live classes, test series, and study material you've enrolled in.
  2. Account Management: To create and manage your student account, track progress, and provide performance analytics.
  3. Communication: To send course-related notifications, class reminders, exam updates, and administrative messages via email, SMS, and WhatsApp.
  4. Payment Processing: To process course fees, issue invoices, and manage refunds.
  5. Personalization: To recommend courses, study material, and test series based on your preparation and performance.
  6. Improvement: To analyze usage patterns and improve our platform, content quality, and user experience.
  7. Legal Compliance: To comply with applicable laws, regulations, and legal processes.
  8. Marketing: To send promotional offers, new course announcements, and newsletter (with your consent; you can opt-out anytime).

4. Information Sharing & Disclosure

We do NOT sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers: With trusted third-party vendors who assist us in operating our platform (payment gateways, SMS/email services, video hosting). These providers are bound by confidentiality agreements.
  • Faculty Members: Your name and academic progress may be visible to faculty for providing personalized mentorship and doubt resolution.
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law, court order, or government regulation.
  • Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred as part of the business.
  • With Your Consent: For any other purpose with your explicit consent.

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information:

  • Encryption: All data transmission is encrypted using SSL/TLS technology (256-bit encryption).
  • Secure Storage: Personal data is stored on secure servers with restricted access.
  • Payment Security: Payment processing is handled by PCI-DSS compliant payment gateways (Razorpay). We do not store full card details.
  • Access Controls: Strict role-based access controls ensure only authorized personnel can access sensitive data.
  • Regular Audits: We conduct periodic security audits and vulnerability assessments.
  • Data Retention: Personal data is retained only as long as necessary for service delivery or as required by law. Upon account deletion request, data is removed within 30 days.

While we strive to protect your data, no method of electronic storage or transmission is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Cookies Policy

Our website uses cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your browsing experience.

6.1 What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help websites remember your preferences, login status, and usage patterns.

6.2 Types of Cookies We Use

  • Essential Cookies: Required for basic website functionality (login, course access, checkout). Cannot be disabled.
  • Functional Cookies: Remember your preferences like language, region, and course filters.
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand how users interact with our website (Google Analytics). Data is anonymized.
  • Marketing Cookies: Used to show relevant ads and measure campaign effectiveness (with consent).

6.3 Managing Cookies

You can control and delete cookies through your browser settings. However, disabling essential cookies may affect website functionality and prevent access to enrolled courses.

7. Your Rights

As a user of Apex Hub, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right to Access: Request a copy of your personal data we hold.
  • Right to Rectification: Correct any inaccurate or incomplete information.
  • Right to Erasure: Request deletion of your personal data ("Right to be Forgotten").
  • Right to Restrict Processing: Limit how we use your data.
  • Right to Data Portability: Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format.
  • Right to Object: Object to processing for direct marketing purposes.
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: Withdraw consent at any time where processing is based on consent.
  • Right to Complain: Lodge a complaint with the relevant data protection authority.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at privacy@apexhub.in. We will respond within 30 days.

9. Refund & Cancellation Policy

9.1 Free Demo & Trial

We offer 3 free demo classes for every course. We strongly recommend attending these demos before enrollment to assess course quality and teaching methodology.

9.2 Refund Timeline

  • Within 7 Days of Batch Start: Full refund (100%) — no questions asked. Refund processed within 7 working days.
  • 8-15 Days of Batch Start: 50% refund of course fees.
  • 16-30 Days of Batch Start: 25% refund of course fees (only if less than 30% of course content accessed).
  • After 30 Days or 30% Content Access: No refund.

9.3 Non-Refundable Items

  • Test series once any test has been attempted
  • Downloadable study material / e-books
  • Crash courses (due to short duration)
  • One-on-one mentorship sessions once conducted

9.4 How to Request Refund

Send an email to support@apexhub.in with subject "Refund Request - [Course Name]" and include your enrollment ID and reason for refund. Refunds are credited to the original payment method.

Refund processing time: 7-10 working days after approval.
